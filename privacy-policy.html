Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website or services related to solar panel installation, green energy maintenance, and consultations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information (Name, Email, Phone Number, Address)
  • Project Details (Energy requirements, property type, location)
  • Technical Information (Browser type, device, IP address, usage patterns)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide personalized solar solutions
  • Generate accurate free quotes based on your requirements
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience
  • Send service updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (only with your consent)

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners solely for the purpose of delivering solar services, such as:

  • Installation partners and licensed contractors
  • Customer support and CRM service providers
  • Legal or regulatory authorities (only when required by law)

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic and optimize your browsing experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement strict security protocols to protect your data. All information is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent or unsubscribe from communications

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their policies independently.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date at the bottom.
